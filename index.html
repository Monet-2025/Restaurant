Our Story

Monet Restaurant is nestled in the heart of Dilijan, where nature's beauty meets culinary artistry. Inspired by the impressionist master Claude Monet, we believe that every dish should be a masterpiece.

Our menu celebrates Armenian culinary traditions with a modern European twist. We source fresh, local ingredients from the surrounding forests and farms, creating dishes that honor both our heritage and innovation.

At Monet, dining is not just a meal—it's an experience that engages all your senses.
